Our Story

A relentless pursuit of coffee perfection, born from a passion for the craft.

The Beginning

CofPro+ started with a simple belief: coffee isn't just a morning routine; it's a sensory luxury that requires meticulous attention at every stage of its journey.

Frustrated by the commoditization of coffee, our founders set out across the globe to build direct relationships with the most skilled artisanal farmers, ensuring we source only the top 1% of beans available.

Our Core Values

✧

Uncompromising Quality

We grade our beans relentlessly, rejecting anything that doesn't meet our exacting standards. Every roast is precision-tested.

✧

The Art of Craft

Roasting is both an art and a science. Our master roasters coax out the unique terroir of every origin, highlighting nuances.

✧

Deep Passion

We are obsessed with coffee. This obsession drives us to constantly innovate, refine, and elevate the standard for premium coffee.

Our Journey

2018

The Idea

CofPro+ is conceived during a trip to the highlands of Ethiopia, recognizing a gap in the luxury coffee market.

2020

First Roastery

We established our state-of-the-art roasting facility, allowing us complete control over the flavor profiles.

2023

Global Expansion

Curating our collection with beans sourced from 12 different countries, solidifying our Signature blends.
